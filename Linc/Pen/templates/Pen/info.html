Linc Pen & Plastics Ltd. is a well-known Indian stationery company that manufactures and sells pens, pencils, markers, notebooks, and other writing products.

Company Overview

      Founded: 1994 Headquarters: Kolkata, West Bengal, India Industry: Stationery and writing instruments Products: Ball pens, gel pens, roller pens, mechanical pencils, markers, notebooks, and related stationery items.
