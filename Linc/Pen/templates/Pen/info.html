Linc Pen & Plastics Ltd.

Linc Pen & Plastics Ltd. is a well-known Indian stationery company that manufactures and sells pens, pencils, markers, notebooks, and other writing products.

Company Overview

  • Founded: 1994
  • Headquarters: Kolkata, West Bengal, India
  • Industry: Stationery and Writing Instruments
  • Products: Ball pens, gel pens, roller pens, mechanical pencils, markers, notebooks

Popular Linc Pen Products

  • Linc Glycer (Gel Pen)
  • Linc Pentonic
  • Linc Ocean Gel
  • Linc Signeta
